Who We Are

This website is operated by DNP Capstone Project Help, an academic writing and editing service for Doctor of Nursing Practice students. References to "we," "us," and "our" in this policy refer to DNP Capstone Project Help. Our website address is https://dnpcapstoneproject.help/.

If you have any questions about this policy or about how we handle your data, contact us at the details on our contact page.

What Data We Collect and Why

Inquiry and order data: When you contact us by the order form, WhatsApp, or email, we collect the information you provide, your name, email address, WhatsApp number if applicable, DNP specialisation track, the component or assignment you need help with, your deadline, and any files or documents you share. We use this information to assign a writer, communicate with you about your order, and deliver completed work. This data is stored for the duration of your order and any associated revision period. We do not retain it beyond what is needed to service your order unless you request that we keep materials for a future order.

Payment data: Payments are processed through secure third-party payment processors. We do not collect or store your card number, bank account details, or full payment credentials. The payment processor provides us with a transaction reference and payment confirmation only.

Analytics data: We use Ahrefs Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Ahrefs Analytics collects anonymised data about pages visited, time on site, and referral source. This data does not identify you personally. See Ahrefs' privacy policy for details of how they handle analytics data.

Live chat data: We use Tawk.to for live chat support. If you use the chat widget on this site, Tawk.to may collect your IP address, browser type, and the messages you send. See Tawk.to's privacy policy for details.

Cookies: This site uses cookies. See our cookie policy for details of which cookies are set and how to manage them.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our legal basis for processing your personal data is as follows:

Contract performance: Processing your name, contact details, order details, and files is necessary to fulfil the service you have requested from us.

Legitimate interests: We process anonymised analytics data to understand how our website is used and to improve it. This processing does not override your privacy interests.

Consent: Where we use non-essential cookies, we rely on your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser cookie settings or using the opt-out mechanisms described in our cookie policy.

How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We share your order details only with the writer assigned to your order and the quality reviewer responsible for checking the work before delivery. Both are bound by the same confidentiality obligations that govern our service.

We use the following third-party services that may process data in connection with your use of this site: Ahrefs Analytics (website analytics), Tawk.to (live chat), and our payment processor (payment processing). Each operates under its own privacy policy and data processing terms.

We do not transfer your data to countries outside the United Kingdom or European Economic Area unless those countries provide an adequate level of data protection or appropriate safeguards are in place.

Your Rights

If you are in the UK or EEA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction: You can ask us to correct inaccurate data we hold about you.

Erasure: You can ask us to delete your personal data where there is no legitimate reason for us to continue processing it.

Restriction: You can ask us to restrict how we process your data in certain circumstances.

Portability: Where we process your data by automated means on the basis of your consent or contract performance, you can request a copy in a commonly used machine-readable format.

Objection: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details on our contact page. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority in your country (in the UK, the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk).

Data Retention

We retain order and communication data for the duration of your order and associated revision period. After the order is complete and the revision window has closed, we delete your files and personal data unless you have asked us to retain materials for a future order. Anonymised analytics data is retained by our analytics provider under their standard retention schedule.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL. Material changes will be noted on this page with an updated effective date.
