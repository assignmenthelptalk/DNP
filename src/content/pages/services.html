DNP Capstone Project Services, All 8 Deliverables

The DNP capstone moves through eight sequential components. Support is available for any individual component or the full sequence from PICOT question through final manuscript defence preparation.

PICOT Question Development: A committee-ready PICOT question with all five elements explicit, Population (specific clinical setting and patient group), Intervention (named protocol or practice change), Comparison (current standard or historical baseline), Outcome (measurable metric with a quantified target), and Time (implementation duration and measurement window). Includes PICOT vs PICo selection guidance for qualitative projects and worked examples across specialisation tracks.

DNP Capstone Proposal (Chapters 1, 2, and 3): Full three-chapter proposal or individual chapter support. Chapter 1 covers background, significance, local quantified problem statement, purpose statement, PICOT question, practice questions, scope, and definition of terms. Chapter 2 covers the theoretical/conceptual framework selection and stage-mapping, literature search methodology with PRISMA flow, JBI or CASP critical appraisal of 15 to 30 included studies, evidence synthesis table, thematic synthesis narrative, and practice gap statement. Chapter 3 covers project design, setting and sample, intervention description, IRB classification rationale, data collection tools with reliability and validity citations, statistical analysis plan with exact tests named, and Gantt chart.

Literature Review: Systematic database search (CINAHL Complete, PubMed/MEDLINE, Cochrane Library, EMBASE, PsycINFO), MeSH and CINAHL heading strategy, PRISMA flow diagram, JBI Critical Appraisal Checklists applied by study design, evidence synthesis table, thematic synthesis narrative, and practice gap statement. Delivered as a standalone document or integrated into Chapter 2.

IRB Protocol: QI non-research determination request or full IRB application, depending on the project classification under 45 CFR 46. Includes the classification rationale using exact federal regulatory language, HIPAA compliance plan, data security plan, and consent or waiver justification. Both university IRB and hospital IRB documents supported.

Implementation Plan: Operational blueprint covering change management framework selection and mapping (Kotter 8-Step, Lewin 3-Stage, or Rogers Diffusion of Innovations), Power-Interest Grid stakeholder analysis, staff education plan with content outline and competency verification threshold (80%), process and outcome measure specification, fidelity monitoring protocol, Gantt chart timeline, and sustainability plan.

Data Collection Tools: Selection and documentation of validated instruments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, MMAS-8, Braden Scale, Columbia SSRS, Morse Fall Scale, and others), EHR-based quality reports (NHSN, NDNQI, CMS Core Measures), and purpose-built compliance audit checklists. Includes reliability and validity reporting and APA 7th edition citation of original psychometric publications.

Data Analysis: Statistical test selection and execution (paired t-test, Wilcoxon signed-rank, McNemar, chi-square), statistical process control chart construction (P-chart, U-chart, I-MR chart, run chart), qualitative thematic or content analysis, SPSS or JASP output interpretation, results tables formatted in APA 7th edition, and statistical vs clinical significance reporting (MCID values for all common DNP outcome instruments).

Final Manuscript: Complete scholarly manuscript (all sections from abstract through appendices) or section-specific support. Past-tense rewrite of the proposal, APA 7th student paper formatting throughout, structured 250-word abstract, results section with tables and SPC charts, discussion with literature comparison and theoretical framework application, implications for practice across four domains (practice, education, policy, leadership), and limitations and recommendations sections.

DNP Capstone Defence Preparation

Proposal defence and final oral defence preparation includes presentation structure guidance (20 to 40 minute committee presentation format), anticipated committee questions by chapter with model responses, revision response matrix template for documenting changes in response to committee feedback, and final defence presentation review.

Non-Capstone Coursework Services

Non-capstone DNP coursework represents a significant portion of the programme, theory papers, health policy analyses, leadership assignments, and epidemiology problem sets all carry their own doctoral-level expectations. Support is available for all of the following course assignment types:

Theory of Nursing Science: Theory analysis papers (concept identification, theoretical framework, empirical referents), theory application papers (applying Watson, Orem, Roy, Kolcaba, or Benner to a practice scenario), and philosophy of nursing science papers comparing metaparadigm frameworks.

Health Policy Analysis: Policy analysis papers using Kingdon's Multiple Streams or Longest's Policy Cycle, policy briefs in executive format (3 to 5 pages), and comparative policy analyses connecting clinical quality gaps to regulatory mandates (Joint Commission NPSGs, CMS Core Measures, state NP scope of practice laws).

Healthcare Quality and Patient Safety: QI methodology papers, root cause analysis assignments, FMEA, IHI Model for Improvement case studies, Donabedian framework applications, and national quality metric analysis papers.

Leadership and Organisational Change: Leadership philosophy papers, change management case analyses, systems thinking papers, strategic plan development, and interprofessional collaboration assignments referencing IOM Future of Nursing 2020-2030 and IPEC core competencies.

Epidemiology and Biostatistics: Study design critique papers, epidemiology calculation problem sets (incidence rate, prevalence, relative risk, odds ratio, NNT, confidence intervals), and population health data analysis assignments.

Healthcare Finance: Cost-benefit analysis papers with ROI calculations, operating budget development, capital budget justification, and financial case papers for quality improvement projects including HAI cost data.

Nursing Informatics: EHR workflow analysis papers, clinical decision support design proposals, health IT policy analyses, and alert fatigue intervention papers.

Population Health: Community health needs assessment papers, SDOH screening tool analyses, health disparity papers using rate ratios and absolute risk differences, and Healthy People 2030 alignment analyses.

Role Synthesis Paper: The culminating DNP role synthesis or scholarly project paper addressing all 10 AACN 2021 Essentials domains with evidence from programme learning and practicum, written at doctoral scholarly level.

Practicum Portfolio and Documentation: Clinical hours verification logs, competency self-assessment tools, reflective practicum journal entries, and portfolio organisation for clinical residency completion.
