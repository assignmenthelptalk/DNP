What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognise your device on return visits and to remember information about how you use the site. Some cookies are set by the website you are visiting; others are set by third-party services the site uses.

Cookies This Site Uses

Ahrefs Analytics: We use Ahrefs Analytics to understand how visitors use this site, which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and where they came from. Ahrefs Analytics sets cookies to collect this data in anonymised form. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Ahrefs' privacy policy for details.

Tawk.to Live Chat: We use Tawk.to to provide the live chat widget on this site. Tawk.to sets cookies to maintain the chat session, remember returning visitors, and support the chat functionality. If you do not use the chat widget, these cookies do not affect your browsing. See Tawk.to's privacy policy for details.

Strictly necessary cookies: Some cookies are required for the site to function, for example, to serve pages correctly or to support secure connections. These cookies do not collect personal data and do not require consent.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, block third-party cookies only, or delete cookies that have already been set. The steps vary by browser:

Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies and site data

Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this site and other sites you visit. Blocking third-party cookies disables Ahrefs Analytics and Tawk.to chat while keeping the site itself functional.
