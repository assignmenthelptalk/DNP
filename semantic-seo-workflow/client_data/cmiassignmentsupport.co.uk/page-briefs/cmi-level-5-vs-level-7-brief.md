## EAV Depth Map

**Five key differences — each must be defined and contrasted:**

| Dimension | CMI Level 5 | CMI Level 7 |
|---|---|---|
| Scope | Team, department, operational function | Organisation, sector, board |
| Format | Management report | Strategic paper |
| Primary command verbs | Evaluate, Justify | Critically Analyse, Justify at strategic depth |
| Academic sources | 10–12 (management textbooks, ManagementDirect) | 15–20 (peer-reviewed journals, empirical research) |
| Word count per unit | 3,000–5,000 words | 5,000–6,500 words |
| Writer credential | Practising manager, CMI L5 qualified | Director/executive, CMI L7 or MBA |
| Student profile | Middle manager, NHS Band 7, operational leader | Director, C-suite, NHS Band 8b+, military officer |
| Qualification equivalence | Foundation Degree / HNC | Master's / MBA equivalent |
| CMI membership | ACMI (Associate Member) | FCMI (Fellow) |
| Typical Diploma word count | 18,000–30,000 words | 35,000–50,000 words |

**Additional comparison dimensions:**
- Is Level 7 harder than Level 5? (yes — qualitatively different, not just quantitatively more)
- Can you skip Level 5 and go straight to Level 7? (yes — if experience and employer sponsor support it)
- What does Level 6 sit between them? (Professional Management — Critically Evaluate, between Evaluate and Critically Analyse)
- How long does each take? (L5 Diploma: 12–24 months; L7 Diploma: 18–36 months)

## FAQ Items (for FAQPage schema)
1. What is the difference between CMI Level 5 and Level 7? → Five key differences: scope, format, command verbs, academic sources, and writer credential
2. Which is harder — CMI Level 5 or Level 7? → Level 7 is qualitatively harder, not just longer — the Critically Analyse command verb requires a different mode of academic engagement
3. Can I go straight to CMI Level 7 without doing Level 5? → Yes — entry to Level 7 is based on experience and employer sponsor decision, not on prior CMI qualification
4. How long does CMI Level 5 take compared to Level 7? → L5 Diploma typically 12–24 months; L7 Diploma typically 18–36 months alongside full-time senior leadership roles
5. What CMI membership do I get from Level 5 vs Level 7? → Level 5 Diploma supports ACMI (Associate Member); Level 7 Diploma supports FCMI (Fellow) — the most prestigious CMI membership grade
6. What is CMI Level 6 and where does it fit? → Level 6 is Professional Management and Leadership — sits between L5 and L7. Command verb is Critically Evaluate (above L5's Evaluate, below L7's Critically Analyse).
