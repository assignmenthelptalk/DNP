### H1: Google Ads in Kenya: Reach Customers Who Are Searching for Your Business Right Now

**Opening paragraph (50–80 words):**
Google Ads places your Kenyan business at the top of Google.co.ke search results the moment a campaign goes live. Unlike SEO, which builds organic rankings over months, Google Ads delivers immediate traffic from Kenyan customers actively searching for your products or services. The trade-off: every click has a cost (CPC — Cost Per Click). The skill is targeting the right Kenyan search queries at the right cost, and sending those clicks to a landing page that converts.

---

### H2: How Google Ads Works for Kenyan Businesses

**EAV depth required:**
- Google Ads auction: every search on Google.co.ke triggers an ad auction — advertisers bid on keywords
- Quality Score: Google rates each ad on relevance, expected CTR, and landing page experience — higher Quality Score = lower CPC for the same position
- Ad rank: determines which ads show and in what position — ad rank = bid × Quality Score
- Kenya-specific: Google Ads in Kenya uses the same auction system as global Google, but CPCs are significantly lower than US/UK markets — making Kenya a relatively affordable paid search market
- Campaign structure: Account → Campaigns (by objective) → Ad Groups (by keyword theme) → Ads + Keywords
- Billing: Google Ads bills in USD by default — Kenyan businesses can pay via M-Pesa (Google Ads Kenyan billing via Safaricom partnership), Visa/Mastercard

**Word count target:** 240–300 words

### H2: Google Ads Campaign Types for Kenyan Businesses

**EAV depth required:**
- Search campaigns: text ads in Google.co.ke search results — best for service businesses (law firms, clinics, agencies, consultants) in Kenya
- Display campaigns: image banner ads on Google partner websites — brand awareness for Kenyan businesses across Kenyan news sites, blogs
- Shopping campaigns: product listings with photos and prices in Google Search — for Kenyan e-commerce businesses
- YouTube/Video campaigns: video ads before YouTube videos — growing Kenyan YouTube audience (20M+ monthly users)
- Performance Max: automated campaign using AI across all Google channels — Google manages placement optimization
- Remarketing: show ads to previous Kenyan website visitors as they browse other Google partner sites — high conversion rate as these users already know the business
- Kenyan use case by campaign type:

| Restaurant Westlands | Local Search + Display |
| E-commerce Kenya | Shopping + Remarketing |

### H2: Targeting Kenyan Audiences With Google Ads

**EAV depth required:**
- Geographic targeting: county-level (Nairobi County, Mombasa County), city-level (Kisumu, Nakuru), radius targeting from a location (within 10km of CBD)
- Keyword targeting for Kenya: include Kenya-specific modifiers in keywords — "website design Nairobi" not just "website design"
- Audience targeting: demographic (age, gender), in-market audiences (Google identifies Kenyans actively researching your category), customer match (upload existing Kenyan customer email list)
- Device targeting: Kenyan Google Ads traffic is 70%+ mobile — bid adjustments recommended (increase bids for mobile if landing page is mobile-optimized)
- Time targeting: Kenya business hours (Mon–Fri 8AM–6PM EAT) typically show best conversion rates for B2B; 6PM–10PM good for B2C
- Language targeting: English primary; Swahili ads can be effective for Kenyan B2C audiences

**Word count target:** 240–300 words

### H2: Conversion Tracking for Kenyan Google Ads Campaigns

**EAV depth required:**
- Why conversion tracking is non-negotiable: without it, you're spending Ksh blind — you know clicks happened but not which clicks became customers
- Conversion actions to track for Kenyan businesses:
  - Contact form submissions
  - Phone call clicks (from mobile — critical for Kenya)
  - WhatsApp link clicks (wa.me link events)
  - M-Pesa payment completions (for e-commerce)
  - Time on site / page depth (proxy for engaged Kenyan visitors)
- Setup: Google Ads conversion tag via Google Tag Manager OR GA4 imported conversions
- Kenya-specific: phone call conversions are undertracked on most Kenyan Google Ads accounts — click-to-call tracking setup is essential as many Kenyan customers call rather than fill forms
- Google Analytics 4 integration: link GA4 to Google Ads — enables audience-based bidding and lifetime value optimization for Kenyan ad campaigns

**Word count target:** 240–300 words
