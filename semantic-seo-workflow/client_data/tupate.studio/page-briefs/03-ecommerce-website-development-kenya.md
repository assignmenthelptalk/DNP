### H2: M-Pesa Integration: The Non-Negotiable for E-commerce in Kenya

**Contextual directive:** This is the most critical section for Kenya-specific differentiation. No business can succeed in Kenyan e-commerce without M-Pesa. Cover it in technical depth that demonstrates Tupate Studio's capability.

**EAV depth required:**
- Entity: M-Pesa Payment Integration
- Attributes:
  - Daraja API: Safaricom's developer API for integrating M-Pesa into websites — the foundation of any Kenyan e-commerce payment system
  - STK Push (Lipa Na M-Pesa Online): customer enters phone number → receives M-Pesa prompt on their phone → approves → payment auto-confirmed to merchant. Most seamless UX.
  - Paybill/Buy Goods: customer manually pays to merchant's paybill number → enters reference code → business confirms manually. Lower UX but works as fallback.
  - C2B (Customer to Business) API: automates payment confirmation without manual checking
  - B2C (Business to Customer) API: enables refunds directly to customer M-Pesa — critical for returns compliance
  - Payment processing timeline: STK Push confirmation in 5–10 seconds; funds in merchant's M-Pesa within 24 hours
  - Daraja API fees: Safaricom transaction fee (% based on amount) — business must factor into pricing
  - Alternative integrations: Pesapal (M-Pesa + Visa/Mastercard + Equity), iPay, Flutterwave — comparison by use case
  - Setup requirement: Safaricom Business account, M-Pesa Till or Paybill number, API credentials — Tupate Studio handles full setup

**Word count target:** 380–450 words

### H2: Payment Gateways Available for Kenyan E-commerce Websites

**Contextual directive:** Beyond M-Pesa, cover the full Kenyan payment gateway ecosystem. This is EAV depth that no shallow competitor covers.

**EAV depth required:**
- Pesapal: aggregates M-Pesa + Visa/Mastercard + Equity Bank + Airtel Money. Best for businesses needing card payment alongside M-Pesa. Integration fee + transaction percentage.
- iPay Africa: M-Pesa + bank cards. Popular with Kenyan NGOs and professional services.
- Flutterwave: pan-African payment gateway — M-Pesa + cards + bank transfers. Good for businesses with cross-border African customers (Uganda, Tanzania).
- Equity Bank PayStar: direct Equity Bank integration for businesses with Equity account
- KCB Mobile Banking API: for businesses with KCB accounts
- Airtel Money: smaller market share than M-Pesa but relevant for certain demographics
- Recommendation by business type:
  - Retail e-commerce: Pesapal (M-Pesa + card in one checkout)
  - B2B/invoicing: direct M-Pesa Paybill + bank EFT
  - Cross-border: Flutterwave
  - Pure M-Pesa: Daraja API direct

### H2: KRA Compliance and VAT for Kenyan E-commerce Businesses

**Contextual directive:** Kenyan business owners fear getting KRA compliance wrong. Addressing this builds authority and demonstrates Tupate Studio understands Kenya's regulatory environment.

**EAV depth required:**
- KRA VAT registration: businesses with annual turnover above Ksh 5 million must register for VAT (16%)
- e-TIMS (eTax Invoice Management System): KRA's electronic invoicing system — e-commerce stores selling to registered businesses must integrate with e-TIMS from 2024 onwards
- VAT display on website: KRA requires VAT-registered businesses to display prices inclusive of VAT and show VAT breakdown in invoices
- KRA PIN display: every business website must display KRA PIN number (legal requirement)
- Consumer Protection Act: returns and refunds policy must comply with Kenya's Consumer Protection Act (Cap 46B)
- Data Protection Act: e-commerce stores collecting customer data (name, phone, email) must comply with Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 — privacy policy required
- What Tupate Studio includes: VAT calculation in checkout, KRA PIN display, privacy policy page, terms and conditions page

**Word count target:** 260–320 words
