## EAV Requirements

### Entity: Performance Management Cycle
- Stages: plan → act → review → develop (cyclical, not linear)
- Planning: agree objectives, development plan, review frequency
- Acting: day-to-day performance, manager support, coaching conversations
- Reviewing: formal appraisal, mid-year check-in, evidence gathering
- Developing: training, coaching, stretch assignments, CPD

### Entity: Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs
- Level 1 Physiological: pay (covers basic needs — food, housing, warmth)
- Level 2 Safety: job security, safe working conditions, contract certainty
- Level 3 Social: team belonging, relationships, collaborative culture
- Level 4 Esteem: recognition, promotion, job title, performance bonuses
- Level 5 Self-actualisation: meaningful work, autonomy, growth opportunities
- HR application: reward must address unmet needs — base pay addresses Level 1–2 before recognition schemes (Level 4) can motivate

### Entity: Herzberg's Two-Factor Theory
- Hygiene factors (prevent dissatisfaction): pay, working conditions, supervision, company policy — removing these causes dissatisfaction but improving them does NOT motivate
- Motivators (create satisfaction): achievement, recognition, responsibility, advancement, the work itself
- HR application: intrinsic reward and job design matter as much as pay; pay alone does not sustain performance

### Entity: Total Reward
- Financial direct: basic pay, overtime, commission, bonus
- Financial indirect: pension, health insurance, company car, annual leave
- Non-financial intrinsic: autonomy, purpose, mastery, interesting work
- Non-financial extrinsic: recognition schemes, flexible working, career development
