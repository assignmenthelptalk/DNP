## EAV Requirements

### Entity: CIPD Wellbeing Dimensions (6)
- Physical: exercise, sleep, nutrition, absence of illness, ergonomics, safety
- Psychological/mental: stress management, resilience, absence of anxiety/depression, psychological safety
- Financial: pay adequacy, financial literacy, pension, debt support
- Social: relationships at work, sense of belonging, team connection, reduced isolation
- Career: job satisfaction, growth opportunity, autonomy, job design quality
- Purpose: meaningful work, alignment with values, contribution beyond self

### Entity: HSE Management Standards (6 work-related stress risk factors)
- Demands: workload, work patterns, work environment
- Control: how much say employees have in how they work
- Support: encouragement and resources from managers and colleagues
- Relationships: promoting positive working to avoid conflict and dealing with unacceptable behaviour
- Role: clarity of role and absence of conflicting roles
- Change: how organisational change is managed and communicated

### Entity: Presenteeism vs Absenteeism
- Absenteeism: days lost to absence — measurable, often tracked but underestimates true cost
- Presenteeism: attending work while unwell — productivity loss while physically present; harder to measure but costs estimated at 1.5× absenteeism cost (CIPD research)
- Combined: "Total lost productivity" = absenteeism + presenteeism — the true cost of poor wellbeing
