## EAV Requirements

### Entity: ADDIE Model
- Analyse: identify learning need, audience, context, constraints
- Design: learning objectives, assessment strategy, content outline, media selection
- Develop: build content assets — e-learning modules, videos, job aids
- Implement: deploy to learners via LMS or facilitated delivery
- Evaluate: formative (during development) and summative (after delivery) — using Kirkpatrick

### Entity: Knowles' Andragogy (Adult Learning Principles)
- Self-concept: adults are self-directed — give learners control over pace and path
- Experience: adults bring rich experience — use it as a resource, not a barrier
- Readiness: adults learn what they need for their current role or life situation
- Orientation: adults are problem-centred, not subject-centred — focus on application
- Motivation: adults are primarily internally motivated — connect learning to personal value
- Need to know: adults need to know why before they will engage

### Entity: Gamification Mechanics
- Points: reward specific actions (completion, quiz performance, contribution)
- Badges: recognise achievement of milestones or skills
- Leaderboards: create social comparison and competition — use with caution (demotivates low performers)
- Narrative/quest: embed learning in a story arc with clear stakes and progression
- Feedback loops: immediate, specific feedback after each action
- Risk: gamification adds engagement only when the underlying content has value — it does not rescue poor content
