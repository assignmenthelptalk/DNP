## EAV Requirements

### Entity: HR Data Types
- Quantitative: numerical, measurable — headcount, turnover rate, absence rate, time-to-hire, cost-per-hire
- Qualitative: descriptive, interpretive — exit interview themes, engagement survey comments, 360 feedback narratives
- Primary: collected directly — surveys, interviews, observations, focus groups
- Secondary: already exists — HR information system (HRIS) records, ONS labour market data, CIPD benchmarking reports

### Entity: Key HR Metrics (examples to use)
- Labour turnover rate: (number of leavers ÷ average headcount) × 100
- Absence rate: (days lost ÷ available working days) × 100
- Time-to-hire: days from job posting to accepted offer
- Cost-per-hire: total recruitment costs ÷ number of hires
- Employee engagement score: survey-derived percentage

### Entity: Data Presentation Methods
- Bar chart: comparing categories (e.g. absence by department)
- Line graph: showing trends over time (e.g. turnover rate month-by-month)
- Table: precise figures for multiple variables
- Narrative summary: written interpretation for non-HR stakeholders
- Dashboard: combined view for HR reporting to senior management
