## EAV Requirements

### Entity: CIPD Level 5 Qualification
**Attributes + Values to cover:**
- Qualification name: Associate Diploma in People Management (not "Level 5 CIPD" alone)
- Awarding body: CIPD (Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development)
- Level on RQF/SCQF: Level 5 (England/Wales), equivalent to second-year undergraduate
- Credit requirement: minimum 120 credits (21 credits per unit × core + pathway + electives)
- Pathway options: People Management (HR), People Development (L&D)
- Assessment format: written portfolio, scenario/case study based, no exams
- Typical duration: 12–18 months part-time
- Target learner: experienced HR/L&D practitioner (2+ years), seeking Associate CIPD membership
- Geographic primary markets: UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Ireland, Qatar, Bahrain

### Entity: Assessment Criteria (ACs)
**Attributes + Values:**
- Structure: each unit has 3–5 ACs, each with sub-criteria
- Pass threshold: all ACs addressed with applied organisational examples + theory connection
- Merit threshold: critical analysis across ≥2 theoretical perspectives, integrated evidence
- Word count per unit: 3,000–4,500 words (varies by unit — specify exact ranges in Supp)
- Format: written portfolio submitted via CIPD assessment platform or via approved centre
