## EAV Requirements

### Entity: Psychological Contract
- Definition: unwritten set of expectations and obligations between employer and employee
- Components: transactional (pay, hours) and relational (trust, fairness, respect)
- Formation: develops from onboarding, manager behaviour, culture signals
- Breach consequences: reduced engagement, absenteeism, resignation, reduced discretionary effort

### Entity: ACAS Code of Practice (Disciplinary)
- Step 1: Investigation — gather facts before any action
- Step 2: Notification — written letter with details of allegation and meeting invitation
- Step 3: Hearing — employee has right to be accompanied (trade union rep or colleague)
- Step 4: Decision — communicated in writing with right of appeal
- Step 5: Appeal — independent manager, same procedural rights
- Note: Failure to follow ACAS Code can increase tribunal awards by up to 25%

### Entity: Conflict Resolution Methods
- Mediation: neutral third party facilitates agreement — voluntary, informal
- Arbitration: neutral third party makes binding decision — more formal
- Conciliation: ACAS conciliation before tribunal — free, non-binding
- Internal resolution: line manager or HR intervention at earliest stage
