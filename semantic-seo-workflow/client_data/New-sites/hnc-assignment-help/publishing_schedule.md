## Sequencing Logic

**Phase 1 — Core Tier 1 (Subject Service Pages + Transition Anchor)**
Establish the primary money pages for the four highest-volume HNC subjects plus the main overview. Critically, the BTEC Level 3 to HNC transition page is published in Phase 1 — it is the single most differentiated asset in this niche and the conceptual spine that links all other content.

**Phase 2 — Core Tier 2A (Grading Intelligence + Academic Foundation)**
Grading criteria, merit/distinction strategy, referencing, and resubmission pages go live immediately after Phase 1. These are the depth differentiators from thin competitors and capture the highest-converting audiences (referred students and grade-improvement seekers).

**Phase 3 — Core Tier 2B (Remaining Subject Pages + Unit Depth)**
Remaining subject cluster pages (Construction, Hospitality, Sport) plus the HNC Business Research Project unit page. These extend Core Section width and create internal link density around Phase 1 subject hubs.

**Phase 4 — Core Tier 2C (Supporting Process + Gap Topics)**
Assignment brief interpretation, academic writing skills, and the Pearson Examiner Reports gap asset. These universally applicable pages reinforce the site's academic depth signal.

**Phase 5 — Outer Tier 1 (Qualification Discovery and Progression)**
HNC overview, comparison, and progression pages capturing prospective students and current students planning next steps.

**Phase 6 — Outer Tier 2 and 3 + Remaining Gap Topics**
Academic integrity, assessment process, professional recognition, part-time study context, career outcomes, and border expansion.
