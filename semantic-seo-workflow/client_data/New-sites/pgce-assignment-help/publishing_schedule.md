---

## Sequencing Logic

**Phase 1 — Core Tier 1 (Route Service Pages)**
Establish the primary money pages for each PGCE route and the main service overview. These capture the highest-intent transactional queries from trainees actively searching for help.

**Phase 2 — Core Tier 2A (Universal Process Pages)**
Reflective writing and Teachers' Standards alignment pages apply to every PGCE student regardless of route or subject. These are the deepest differentiators from thin competitors and must be indexed early to support all subsequent pages.

**Phase 3 — Core Tier 2B (Assignment Topic Pages)**
The most commonly assessed PGCE assignment topics — behaviour management, assessment for learning, SEND, lesson study, subject knowledge audit, and curriculum design. Published in order of assessment frequency across PGCE programmes.

**Phase 4 — Core Tier 2C (Subject-Specific and Referral Pages)**
Subject pedagogy pages for English and Maths (highest-volume PGCE subjects) and the referral recovery page, which captures the highest-conversion audience.

**Phase 5 — Outer Tier 1 (Qualification Discovery and Comparison)**
Route overview, comparison, and entry requirements pages capturing prospective trainees earlier in the decision journey.

**Phase 6 — Outer Tier 2 and 3 + Gap Topics (Trust, Knowledge Base, and Border Expansion)**
Teachers' Standards complete guide, theory knowledge base pages, academic integrity, Harvard referencing, career context, and the high-priority gap topics.

- Every Core Tier 1 route page links to: PGCE Assignment Help (main), PGCE Reflective Writing Help, Teachers' Standards in PGCE Assignments, and the subject-specific pages relevant to that route
- PGCE Reflective Writing Help links to: all four Core Tier 1 route pages and Teachers' Standards in PGCE Assignments
