## Sequencing Logic

**Phase 1 — Core Tier 1 (Qualification Service Pages)**
Establish the primary money pages for each NEBOSH qualification type first. These capture the highest-intent, transactional queries. Google must index and evaluate these before outer content links to them.

**Phase 2 — Core Tier 2A (Grading and Strategy Pages)**
Marking criteria, model answers, and first-attempt strategy pages create the unique depth signal that separates this site from thin competitors. These also capture students at the moment of highest conversion intent (about to submit or just referred).

**Phase 3 — Core Tier 2B (Specialist Qualification Pages)**
Fire, Environmental, and Construction certificate assignment help pages expand the Core Section subject coverage. Lower volume than IGC/NGC but zero-competition opportunities.

**Phase 4 — Outer Tier 1 (Qualification Discovery)**
Overview and comparison pages capture students earlier in the qualification journey and funnel them toward Core Section service pages.

**Phase 5 — Outer Tier 2 (Knowledge Foundation Pages)**
Health and safety content that directly supports NEBOSH assignment content — risk assessment, legislation, ISO 45001. These pages build contextual neighborhood cohesion and propagate trust to Core pages.

**Phase 6 — Outer Tier 3 + Gap Topics (Border Expansion)**
Career context, comparison, and unique depth assets. Gap topics including referral recovery and examiner report analysis close the border.
