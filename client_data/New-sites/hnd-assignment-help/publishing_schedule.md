## Sequencing Logic

**Phase 1 — Core Tier 1 (Subject Service Pages)**
Establish the primary money pages for the four highest-volume HND subjects plus the main overview page. These capture transactional queries from students actively enrolled and searching for help now.

**Phase 2 — Core Tier 2A (Grading Intelligence Pages)**
Distinction criteria, achievement strategy, and resubmission pages go live immediately after Phase 1. These are the depth differentiators that separate this site from thin single-page competitors and capture the highest-converting audience (referred students and distinction seekers).

**Phase 3 — Core Tier 2B (Remaining Subject Pages and Unit Depth)**
Remaining subject cluster pages (Hospitality, Construction, Sport, Early Childhood) plus the HND Business Research Project deep unit page. These extend Core Section width and create dense internal linking around the Phase 1 subject hubs.

**Phase 4 — Core Tier 2C (Supporting Process Pages)**
Assignment brief interpretation, Harvard referencing guide, and the critical thinking skills page. These universally applicable pages support all subject clusters and reinforce the site's academic depth signal.

**Phase 5 — Outer Tier 1 (Qualification Discovery and Progression)**
HND overview, comparison, and progression pages capturing students earlier in the journey and funnelling them toward Core Section service pages.

**Phase 6 — Outer Tier 2 and 3 + Gap Topics (Trust, Context, and Border Expansion)**
Academic integrity, assessment process, professional recognition, international students, and the high-priority gap topics (mature students, Pearson Examiner Reports, distinction portfolio).
