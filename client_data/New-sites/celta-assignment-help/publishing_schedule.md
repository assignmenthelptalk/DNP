## Sequencing Logic

**Phase 1 — Core Tier 1 (Assignment-Specific Service Pages + Language Analysis Anchor)**
Establish the five primary money pages — the main overview and one page per CELTA written assignment — plus CELTA Language Analysis Help, which is elevated into Phase 1 because it is the technical spine connecting Assignments 1 and 2. All five assignment pages must be indexed before the grammar analysis and IPA depth pages go live so they receive internal links immediately.

**Phase 2 — Core Tier 2A (Language Analysis Technical Depth)**
Grammar analysis, phonology and IPA, Pass B strategy, referral and resubmission guide, and lesson planning go live in rapid succession. These are the depth differentiators from the handful of blog posts that constitute current competition — and they capture the highest-converting audiences: referred candidates (resubmission guide) and candidates targeting the Pass B grade (Pass B strategy page).

**Phase 3 — Core Tier 2B (Worked Example Grammar Pages + Supporting Core)**
The three high-priority worked example gap assets (present perfect, modal verbs, conditionals) are published as a cluster in Phase 3. These are the highest-specificity content assets in the CELTA niche — no competitor provides full form-meaning-use-phonology analysis at this depth for specific grammar items. Publishing them as a cluster reinforces Grammar Analysis page authority through internal linking. Online blended and academic referencing pages close Phase 3.

**Phase 4 — Core Tier 2C (Gap Topics + CCQ and Error Analysis Technique Pages)**
Career changers gap page, lexis analysis gap page, and two Outer Tier 2 technique pages (CCQs and error analysis) elevated into Phase 4 because of their direct Assignment 1 and Assignment 2 linkage. International candidates gap closes Phase 4.

**Phase 5 — Outer Tier 1 (Qualification Discovery and Comparison)**
CELTA overview, qualification comparisons, delivery route comparison, and pass rate pages capturing prospective candidates earlier in the decision journey. Published from late July through August — the primary window when September intake candidates research CELTA.

**Phase 6 — Outer Tier 2 and 3 + Remaining Gap Topics**
Academic integrity, teaching practice connection, EFL methodology, and contextual border/outer knowledge pages closing the content network.
