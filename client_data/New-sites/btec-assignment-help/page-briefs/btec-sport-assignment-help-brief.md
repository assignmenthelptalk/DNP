## New H2 Section Specification

### H2: BTEC Sport HNC/HND: Sports Development, Management, and Research Requirements
> **EAV to cover**: HNC Sport unit structure, mandatory Harvard referencing, HND research project equivalence, academic sourcing standards for sport science, what Distinction looks like at Level 4 and Level 5
> **Word count**: 300–360 words
> **Specific content**:
> - **HNC Sport (Level 4) unit structure**: core units include Sports Development (community sport delivery, funding models, National Governing Body policy), Sports Management (facility management, event planning, financial management in sport), Health-Related Exercise and Fitness (exercise prescription theory, ACSM guidelines application), Research Methods in Sport (quantitative and qualitative research design, data collection methodology), and Psychology of Sport (arousal, motivation, team dynamics, mental skills training). Harvard referencing is mandatory for all written assignments from Level 4 onwards
> - **HND Sport (Level 5) Research Project**: the HND equivalent of the Managing a Successful Business Project is typically a Sport and Exercise Research Project or dissertation requiring a minimum 5,000-word structured report. The report structure mirrors academic research: introduction and rationale, literature review (minimum 10–15 peer-reviewed sources), research methodology (design, sample, data collection instruments, ethical considerations), results, discussion, and conclusions with evidence-based recommendations. Ethics approval from the college research ethics committee is typically required before data collection can begin
> - **Academic sourcing for sport science at HNC/HND**: acceptable Level 4/5 sources include peer-reviewed journals (Journal of Strength and Conditioning Research, British Journal of Sports Medicine, International Journal of Sport Science, Journal of Applied Sport Psychology, European Journal of Sport Science) and professional guidelines (ACSM, British Dietetic Association, BASES). Textbooks provide foundational support; journal articles are expected for Distinction criteria in analytical and evaluative sections. Wikipedia and general sport websites are not acceptable as academic sources at HNC level or above
> - **Distinction at HNC/HND**: the shift from National-level Distinction (justified evaluative judgement using course materials) to HNC Distinction requires the same judgement to be supported by published research evidence. At HND Level 5, Distinction requires engaging with conflicting research — acknowledging where the evidence base is contested, evaluating the methodological quality of the studies cited, and reaching a justified position despite the ambiguity
