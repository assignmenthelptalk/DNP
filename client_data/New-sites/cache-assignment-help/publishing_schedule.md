## Sequencing Logic

**Phase 1 — Core Tier 1 (Qualification Service Pages + Format Anchors)**
Establish the primary money pages for each CACHE level and route, plus the two most unique CACHE format pages (observation records and reflective accounts). These format pages are elevated into Phase 1 because they are CACHE-specific assets with no competitor equivalent and must be indexed early to receive internal links from all service pages.

**Phase 2 — Core Tier 2A (Theory and Framework Depth)**
Child development theory, EYFS framework application, and safeguarding pages go live immediately after Phase 1. These are the content areas most directly assessed in CACHE assignments and the deepest differentiators from generic childcare writing resources.

**Phase 3 — Core Tier 2B (Topic-Specific Assignment Pages)**
SEND and inclusion, health and safety, portfolio evidence guide, and the referral recovery page extend Core Section coverage and create internal link density around the Phase 1 service hubs.

**Phase 4 — Core Tier 2C (Level 4/5 + Gap Topics)**
CACHE Level 4/5 assignment help and the high-priority gap topics (Level 2-to-3 transition, NCFE assessor feedback analysis, EDI dedicated page).

**Phase 5 — Outer Tier 1 (Qualification Discovery and Comparison)**
CACHE overview, qualification comparison, and EYE status pages capturing prospective students earlier in the decision journey.

**Phase 6 — Outer Tier 2 and 3 + Remaining Gap Topics**
Academic integrity, portfolio assessment process, supporting knowledge pages, career context, and remaining border expansion.
