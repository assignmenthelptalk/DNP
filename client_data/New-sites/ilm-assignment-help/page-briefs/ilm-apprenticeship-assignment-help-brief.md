## EAV Depth Map
**Primary Entity**: ILM assignment within management apprenticeship programmes

**Derived Attributes**:
- **Apprenticeship Standards**: Team Leader/Supervisor (Level 3 Apprenticeship Standard, equivalent to ILM Level 3); Operations/Departmental Manager (Level 5 Apprenticeship Standard, equivalent to ILM Level 5); Senior Leader (Level 7 Apprenticeship Standard, equivalent to ILM Level 7).
- **ILM as EPA Preparation Vehicle**: ILM qualification is often embedded within an apprenticeship as the academic component — completing the ILM qualification provides the knowledge and evidence base for EPA. The ILM certificate/diploma is not the apprenticeship itself — the apprenticeship culminates in EPA (End Point Assessment).
- **Portfolio Requirements**: Apprenticeship portfolios must show 20% off-the-job training evidence alongside work-based knowledge and skills evidence. Portfolio entries must directly map to the Knowledge, Skills, and Behaviours (KSBs) in the apprenticeship standard — not just to ILM unit criteria.
- **KSB Mapping**: Every piece of evidence must be mapped to specific Knowledge, Skills, and Behaviours in the apprenticeship standard. Dual mapping (ILM criteria + KSBs) is required for most submissions.
- **Gateway Requirements**: Before EPA, apprentices must meet gateway criteria: completed all ILM units, employer confirmation of competence, portfolio reviewed and signed off by training provider, English and maths qualifications (if not previously held).
- **EPA Format**: Independent professional discussion (structured interview with independent assessor reviewing portfolio). Some standards also require a work-based project report. EPA is graded Pass/Distinction (some standards) or Pass/Fail.
- **Distinction in EPA**: Requires demonstrating impact beyond the immediate team — broader organisational contribution, strategic awareness, independent judgement in complex situations.

**Values per Attribute**:
- Level 3 apprenticeship: Team Leader/Supervisor Standard
- Level 5 apprenticeship: Operations/Departmental Manager Standard
- Level 7 apprenticeship: Senior Leader Standard
- Portfolio: 20% off-the-job training evidence required
- Gateway: ILM completion + employer sign-off + English/maths
- EPA type: Professional discussion + (some standards) project report
- EPA grading: Pass/Distinction or Pass/Fail (standard-dependent)
