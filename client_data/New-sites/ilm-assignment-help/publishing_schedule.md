## Sequencing Logic

**Phase 1 — Core Tier 1 (Qualification Service Pages)**
Establish the primary money pages for each ILM level and the main service overview. These capture the highest-intent transactional queries. All subsequent pages link back to these.

**Phase 2 — Core Tier 2A (Assessment Process Pages)**
Reflective account writing and work-based evidence guides are published immediately after Core Tier 1 because they support every ILM assignment regardless of level. These are the deepest differentiators from thin competitors.

**Phase 3 — Core Tier 2B (Unit-Specific Depth Pages)**
Level-specific unit pages (Level 3 team leader units, Level 5 leadership and management units, Level 7 strategic units) expand the Core Section depth and create internal link density around the Tier 1 qualification pages.

**Phase 4 — Core Tier 2C (Supporting Process and Theory Pages)**
Leadership theory reference page, Harvard referencing guide, assignment referral and resubmission guide, and Level 4 fill in the remaining Core depth.

**Phase 5 — Outer Tier 1 (Qualification Discovery and Comparison)**
Overview and comparison pages capturing students earlier in the decision journey and funnelling them toward Core service pages.

**Phase 6 — Outer Tier 2 and 3 + Gap Topics (Trust, Context, and Border Expansion)**
Academic integrity, assessment process, career outcomes, and the high-priority gap topics (apprenticeship EPA, public sector).
