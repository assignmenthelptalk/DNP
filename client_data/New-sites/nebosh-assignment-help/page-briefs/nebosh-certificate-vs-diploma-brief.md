- **Certificate Assignment Type**: OBE (48-hour window, 15 scenario-based tasks, command word answers); practical risk assessment report (IG2/NG2). No academic referencing requirement. Answers graded on scenario application, command word compliance, and H&S knowledge accuracy.
- **Diploma Assignment Type**: Extended written academic assignments submitted to NEBOSH. DN1 unit: health and safety management system analysis — academic report format. Academic referencing mandatory (Harvard or similar). Critical analysis and management-level recommendation required.

All three units must be passed to achieve the Diploma.

- **IOSH Membership (Diploma)**: GradIOSH (Graduate Member) — the primary academic route to GradIOSH; thereafter CMIOSH (Chartered Member) via professional review + experience

### FAQ SECTION

**Q: Is the NEBOSH Diploma much harder than the NEBOSH Certificate?**
The Diploma is at RQF Level 6 — three qualification levels above the Certificate (Level 3) — and requires degree-equivalent academic work. It is substantially more demanding than the Certificate in terms of assignment length, academic referencing requirements, critical analysis standard, and study time. However, it is not "harder" in the sense of being inaccessible — it requires a different type of work, and candidates with sufficient H&S experience and academic writing ability can achieve it through structured study.

**Q: Do I need the NEBOSH Certificate before studying the Diploma?**
The NEBOSH Diploma does not formally require the Certificate as a prerequisite — candidates can enrol in the Diploma without holding a certificate. However, the applied management content of the Diploma units, particularly DN3, benefits significantly from prior H&S practice experience. Most Diploma candidates hold a certificate and have several years of H&S work experience before beginning Diploma study.

**Q: What jobs require the NEBOSH Diploma?**
Senior H&S practitioner roles, H&S manager positions, H&S consultancy, and roles that specify GradIOSH or CMIOSH membership typically require the NEBOSH Diploma or equivalent. At board level or in large organisations, Diploma-level qualification combined with CMIOSH membership is often the professional benchmark for lead H&S roles.

**Q: How long does the NEBOSH Diploma take to complete?**
Most working professionals complete the NEBOSH Diploma in 12–18 months, though 24 months is common for those managing high-demand employment alongside study. Each of the three units (DN1, DN2, DN3) is a major academic assignment requiring substantial research, workplace analysis, and extended written work — the time commitment per unit is significantly greater than the OBE preparation required for a Certificate unit.
