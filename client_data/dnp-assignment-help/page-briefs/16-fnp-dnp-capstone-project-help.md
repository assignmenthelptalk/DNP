### FAQ SECTION

**Q: What are the best FNP DNP capstone topics?**
The highest-value FNP capstone topics address chronic disease management problems with measurable quality metric baselines available in the EHR. Strong options include: hypertension management protocol implementation, diabetes self-management education program evaluation, PHQ-9 depression screening protocol standardisation, influenza vaccine compliance improvement, and opioid prescribing guideline adherence projects. The best topic is one where the student has clinical site access, EHR data availability, and a clear gap between current performance and an evidence-based benchmark.

**Q: What EHR data is typically used for an FNP DNP capstone?**
FNP capstone projects most commonly use existing EHR-generated quality reports as the data source — eliminating the need for new data collection and simplifying the IRB determination. Common data sources include: Epic or Athena population health dashboards (for chronic disease control rates), immunisation registry reports (for vaccine compliance), PDMP check logs (for opioid prescribing compliance), and PHQ-9 completion rate reports from the EHR screening module.
