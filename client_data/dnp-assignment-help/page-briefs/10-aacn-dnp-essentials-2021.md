### FAQ SECTION

**Q: What changed between the 2006 and 2021 AACN DNP Essentials?**
The 2006 DNP Essentials outlined 8 role-specific Essentials for DNP programs. The 2021 revision replaced these with a unified 10-domain competency framework that applies across all levels of nursing education — from BSN through DNP — with doctoral-level expectations specified for DNP students. The 2021 framework emphasises competency-based education and requires explicit mapping of program outcomes to domain sub-competencies.

**Q: Which AACN Essentials domains are most important for a DNP capstone?**
Domain 4 (Scholarship for Nursing Practice) is the most central — it encompasses the EBP and QI methodology that defines the capstone project. Domain 5 (Quality and Safety) directly aligns with QI and EBP implementation projects. Domain 7 (Systems-Based Practice) covers the implementation plan, stakeholder engagement, and sustainability planning. Domain 8 (Informatics and Healthcare Technologies) is particularly relevant for data-driven or EHR-based capstones.
