## EAV Depth Map
**Primary Entity**: PMHNP DNP Capstone Project

**Main Attributes**: clinical settings, capstone topics, validated instruments, PICOT examples, IRB considerations, EBP frameworks

**Derived Attributes**:
- **Clinical Settings**: inpatient psychiatric unit, community mental health center (CMHC), outpatient behavioral health clinic, correctional mental health facility, school-based mental health program, integrated primary-behavioral health care (co-located PMHNP in primary care), telehealth behavioral health platform
- **High-Value Capstone Topics**:
  - Depression screening protocol using PHQ-9 in primary care or ED settings
  - Anxiety management using GAD-7 screening + brief CBT protocol
  - PTSD screening protocol using PCL-5 in VA or community mental health settings
  - Medication adherence improvement for patients with schizophrenia (antipsychotic refill rates, MMAS-8 adherence scale)
  - Suicide risk assessment standardisation using Columbia SSRS in ED
  - Naloxone distribution and opioid overdose education protocol (OUD populations)
  - Collaborative care model (CoCM) implementation in primary care
  - Trauma-informed care training program for staff
  - Motivational interviewing training for substance use disorder counselling
- **Validated Instruments**: PHQ-9 (depression, MCID=5 points), GAD-7 (anxiety, MCID=4 points), PCL-5 (PTSD, score ≥33 probable diagnosis), AUDIT-C (alcohol use screening), CAGE-AID (substance use screening), Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale (C-SSRS), MMAS-8 (medication adherence)
- **IRB Considerations**: mental health populations are considered vulnerable under 45 CFR 46.111 — may require expedited or full board review even for QI projects. Prisoners, minors in school settings, and individuals with acute psychiatric illness require additional protections. Suicide risk documentation and safety protocols must be described in the IRB protocol.
- **EBP Frameworks**: Iowa Model (most common), i-PARIHS (for implementation in complex organisational contexts like integrated care), KTA Framework (for knowledge-to-action in community mental health)

### FAQ SECTION

**Q: What are the best PMHNP DNP capstone topics?**
High-impact PMHNP DNP capstone topics include implementing standardised depression screening using the PHQ-9 in primary care or ED settings, improving antipsychotic medication adherence in outpatient schizophrenia patients, implementing the Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale for standardised suicide risk assessment, and implementing collaborative care model components for depression management in primary care. The best topic addresses a measurable gap in mental health care delivery at the student's clinical site.

**Q: Does a PMHNP DNP project always need IRB approval?**
Mental health populations are classified as vulnerable under federal regulations, which means PMHNP capstone projects may require expedited or full board IRB review even if they would qualify as QI non-research in other settings. Students should not self-classify PMHNP projects as QI non-research without first consulting their university IRB. Projects involving suicidal ideation, substance use, or patients with acute psychiatric illness always require formal IRB review.
