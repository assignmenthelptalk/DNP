## EAV Requirements

### Entity: PESTLE
- Political: government policy, trade agreements, political stability
- Economic: inflation, unemployment, GDP growth, interest rates
- Social: demographics, workforce trends, cultural shifts
- Technological: automation, digital transformation, AI
- Legal: employment law, GDPR, Health and Safety at Work Act 1974
- Environmental: net zero targets, sustainability requirements, ESG

### Entity: Handy's Culture Types
- Power culture: centralised decisions, few rules, fast-moving, spider's web structure
- Role culture: bureaucratic, formal hierarchy, rules-based, column and beam structure
- Task culture: project-based, cross-functional teams, flexible, net structure
- Person culture: individual expertise valued, loose structure, self-managing

### Entity: Lewin's Three-Stage Model
- Unfreeze: challenge existing beliefs and prepare for change
- Change: implement new processes, structures, or behaviours
- Refreeze: embed new ways of working as the new normal
