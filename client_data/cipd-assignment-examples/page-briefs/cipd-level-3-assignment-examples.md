## EAV Requirements

### Entity: CIPD Level 3 Qualification
**Attributes + Values:**
- Qualification name: Foundation Certificate in People Practice
- Awarding body: CIPD
- Level on RQF/SCQF: Level 3 (England/Wales), equivalent to A-Level standard
- Credit requirement: minimum 120 credits
- Pathway options: People Practice (HR-focused), People Development (L&D-focused)
- Assessment format: written portfolio and activities, practically-framed scenarios
- Typical duration: 9–12 months part-time
- Target learner: HR/L&D beginners, career changers into people practice, those supporting HR functions
- No prior HR qualification required; some approved centres allow school leavers

### Entity: Level 3 Assessment Criteria
**Attributes + Values:**
- Tone: practical, applied, operational
- Length: 1,500–2,500 words per unit (shorter than Level 5)
- Pass threshold: all ACs addressed, applied to an organisational context
- No merit/distinction at Level 3 — pass/refer only
- Evidence expectation: one or two theoretical frameworks applied operationally, not compared critically
