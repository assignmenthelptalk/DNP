## EAV Requirements

### Entity: CIPD Level 7 Qualification
**Attributes + Values:**
- Qualification name: Advanced Diploma in Strategic People Management
- Awarding body: CIPD
- Level on RQF/SCQF: Level 7 (England/Wales), equivalent to post-graduate/Master's level
- CIPD membership outcome: Chartered Member (MCIPD) pathway — highest individual CIPD grade
- Credit requirement: minimum 120 credits at Level 7
- Pathway options: People Management (strategic HR), People Development (strategic L&D)
- Assessment format: extended written assignments, academic referencing mandatory (Harvard or author-date), often 5,000–7,000+ words per unit
- Typical duration: 18–24 months part-time (sometimes 36 months via distance learning)
- Target learner: experienced HR/L&D director, senior BP, Head of HR — typically 5+ years post-Level 5 or equivalent experience route
- Geographic primary markets: UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar — particularly UAE and Saudi where CIPD Level 7 is a senior HR credential tied to professional licensing

### Entity: Level 7 Assessment Standard
**Attributes + Values:**
- Academic referencing: mandatory — Harvard, APA, or author-date (no informal citations)
- Word count per unit: 5,000–7,500 words (varies by unit)
- Pass threshold: all ACs addressed at post-graduate depth, strategic framing, minimum 8–12 academic sources per unit
- Merit/distinction equivalent: not applicable in same sense — CIPD Level 7 assessed as pass/refer, but quality markers include: synthesis across ≥3 theoretical paradigms, original critical stance, application to real strategic context
- Distinction between describing and critiquing strategy: Level 7 requires the candidate to evaluate strategic HR decisions, not describe them
