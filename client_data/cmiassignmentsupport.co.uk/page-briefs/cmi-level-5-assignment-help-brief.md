##### H3: The Management Report Format at Level 5 — What to Include
> **Attribute + Value**: Title page + Executive Summary (250–300 words, written last) + Contents + Introduction + Analysis sections (theory applied with Evaluate) + Conclusion + Recommendations (3+ SMART, justified) + Bibliography (Harvard, 10–12 sources) + Appendices
> **Writer note**: This breakdown is the most useful format guide for Level 5 students. Name each section with word count expectation where applicable.

##### H3: Evaluate and Justify — The Core Command Verbs at Level 5
> **Attribute + Value**:
> - Evaluate → apply criteria to weigh strengths/weaknesses of a theory, approach, or management decision → requires criteria framework (competency assessment, SMART goals) → not just "pros and cons"
> - Justify → provide evidence-backed reasoning for a chosen approach → cannot be opinion — must be supported by theory and organisational data
> - Develop a Plan → create a specific, actionable plan, often with milestones, resources, and accountability — SMART format required
> **Writer note**: Level 5 students frequently use Evaluate as synonymous with Analyse (Level 4). They are not the same — Evaluate requires CRITERIA, not just component analysis. Make this explicit.
