##### H3: CMI 301 — Principles of Management and Leadership
> Unit overview: Covers the fundamental principles of managing and leading in an organisational context. Commonly essay format, 1,500–2,000 words. Command verbs: Identify, Describe, Explain the principles of management and leadership styles.

##### H3: CMI 302 — Managing a Team to Achieve Results
> Unit overview: Focuses on team management principles and approaches. Covers team roles (Belbin), performance expectations, and team communication. Essay or report format, 1,500–2,000 words.

##### H3: CMI 303 — Managing Individuals to Be Effective in the Workplace
> Unit overview: Examines individual performance management, motivation theories (Maslow, Herzberg), and communication approaches. Essay format, 1,500–2,000 words.

##### H3: CMI 304 — Principles of Communication in the Workplace
> Unit overview: Communication models, barriers to communication, and adapting communication style to different audiences. Short management report or essay, 1,500–2,000 words.

##### H3: CMI 305 — Building Stakeholder Relationships
> Unit overview: Stakeholder identification, analysis, and relationship management. Applies stakeholder theory to a management context. Essay or report, 1,500–2,000 words.

##### H3: CMI 306 — Principles of Delivering Coaching and Mentoring
> Unit overview: Coaching and mentoring models, the difference between coaching and mentoring, and how to apply each in a first-line management context.

##### H3: CMI 307 — Developing the Knowledge, Skills and Abilities of Individuals and Teams
> Unit overview: Covers training needs analysis, CPD approaches, and developing people within a team. Applies learning theorists (Kolb, Honey and Mumford).

##### H3: CMI 308 — Understanding Innovation and Change
> Unit overview: Change management at the operational level. Covers drivers of change, resistance to change, and implementation approaches (Lewin's change model).

##### H3: CMI 309 — Leading Equality, Diversity and Inclusion
> Unit overview: Legal and organisational requirements for EDI. Practical approaches to leading an inclusive team environment.

##### H3: CMI 310 — Understanding Finance
> Unit overview: Introduction to management finance — budgets, cost centres, financial terminology for first-line managers. No advanced financial analysis required.

##### H3: CMI 311 — Contributing to the Delivery of a Project
> Unit overview: Project management fundamentals — scope, planning, stakeholder involvement, milestones. Practical report or project plan format.

##### H3: CMI 312 — Managing Daily Activities
> Unit overview: Operational planning, workload prioritisation, time management, and resource allocation at the supervisory level.
